Wichita Airport (ICT)
Wichita Airport is about a 15 minute drive to and from downtown. Taking an Uber or Lyft from the airport: exit the airport's single terminal, cross the street and walk left until you see Uber and Lyft signs. Drivers typically wait here to drive passengers. Initiate the service through the app for a ride to downtown for about $15-$20. The Fairfield Inn may provide a shuttle if enough guests are booked through our wedding.
